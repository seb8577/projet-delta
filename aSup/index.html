WILD ANIMALS TANZANIA

Tanzania is a big, very big favorite. To see and observe wild animals in their natural environment is a real pleasure. From the king of animals, the lion to the imposing elephant, to the terrifying crocodile of the nile, it was a magical experience. But it was in the Serengeti that I finally got to meet the cheetah in the wild, my dear animal.

LANDSCAPES TANZANIA

My first photo safari in 4x4 mode and on foot. The journey began with the discovery of Tarangire then direction the splendid and immense Serengeti in bivouac mode to the sound of hyenas. Then, immerse yourself in the mythical Ngorongoro crater before ending with a walking safari in Arusha Park. Eyeful !

WILD ANIMALS SOUTH AFRICA

First real trip to the African continent with the aim of seeing the big five, missing the leopard on the clock. The trip was rich in animal encounters: the black pinguins of Cape Town, the great white shark in Gansbaii, the superb meeting with the cheetah Breed River without forgetting the zebra, elephants, kudu, sea lions ... my passion takes shape.
